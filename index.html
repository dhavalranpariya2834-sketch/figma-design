Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does LeadCRM do?

LeadCRM is a browser extension that seamlessly connects your LinkedIn account to your CRM. It eliminates manual data entry by allowing you to instantly sync entire LinkedIn profiles to your CRM in a single click.

Which CRMs do you integrate with?

We offer deep integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho CRM, Google Sheets, Copper, Gohighlevel, Close and Pipedrive. Our two-way sync keeps data flowing seamlessly between LinkedIn and your CRM.

Can I control what information gets synced?

Yes, you have complete control. Our flexible field mapping allows you to precisely define which data from a LinkedIn profile goes into which specific field in your CRM.

Is LeadCRM safe and GDPR compliant?

Absolutely. LeadCRM is built with enterprise-grade security practices and is fully GDPR compliant to protect your data and your customers' data.
